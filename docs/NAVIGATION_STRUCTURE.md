# Navigation Structure

The shell has exactly these top-level sections:

1. Executive Command Center
2. Live Factory Map
3. Orders & Planning
4. Line Assignment Center
5. Material & WIP Readiness
6. Production Execution
7. Downtime & Maintenance
8. Quality & Shipment Risk
9. Management Action Center
10. Reports & Imports
11. Settings / Admin

Routes:

- `/login`
- `/app`
- `/app/executive-command-center`
- `/app/live-factory-map`
- `/app/live-factory-map/[groupCode]`
- `/app/orders-planning`
- `/app/orders-planning/line-assignment`
- `/app/material-wip-readiness`
- `/app/production-execution`
- `/app/downtime-maintenance`
- `/app/quality-shipment-risk`
- `/app/management-action-center`
- `/app/reports-imports`
- `/app/settings-admin`

The app intentionally avoids duplicate dashboard pages and broad page sprawl.
